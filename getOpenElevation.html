Austrian Elevation Service

The "Austrian Elevation Service" is a prototype of a free and open elevation service basing on a DEM with 10 Meter resolution. The service is basing on many textfiles with elevation data saved in EPSG: 3857. The advantage of this approach is that every programming language can read textfiles.
In the source code you find an example how to get elvation data with Javascript.
